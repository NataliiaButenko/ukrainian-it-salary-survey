# Ukrainian IT Salary Survey — December 2022

Exploratory data analysis of the DOU.ua salary survey for Ukrainian IT professionals, conducted in December 2022.

## Project Overview

This project analyzes salary data from over 13,000 Ukrainian IT specialists collected by DOU.ua in December 2022.

The analysis explores salary patterns across:
- job titles,
- years of experience,
- English proficiency,
- gender,
- and regions of Ukraine.

## Project Goal

To uncover patterns in IT compensation across specializations, seniority levels, English proficiency, and gender — providing actionable insights for anyone navigating a career in Ukrainian IT.

## Dataset

- **Source:** [DOU.ua](https://dou.ua) — Ukraine's largest IT community platform
- **Period:** December 2022
- **Size:** 13,315 respondents, 28 features
- **Dataset Type:** Survey data
- **Key fields:** salary (USD/month, net), title, specialization, English level, experience, age, gender, region

## Key Questions

1. How do salaries differ across seniority levels and specializations?
2. Does English proficiency significantly impact compensation?
3. Is there a gender pay gap in Ukrainian IT?
4. How does salary grow with years of experience?
5. Which regions offer the highest compensation?

---

## Key Insights

- Median salary in the dataset is approximately **2600 USD/month**.
- Salary distribution is strongly right-skewed due to high-income outliers.
- Architect, Tech Lead, and Head roles have the highest salaries.
- English proficiency has a strong positive relationship with salary.
- Salary grows rapidly during the first 5–6 years of experience.
- Kyiv remains one of the highest-paying IT regions in Ukraine.
- A gender pay gap is present across most seniority levels.
